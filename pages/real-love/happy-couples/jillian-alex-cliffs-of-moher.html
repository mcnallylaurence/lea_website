Their Story

A sunrise ceremony at the edge of the world, just the two of them.

Jillian and Alex wanted something truly unforgettable — a moment just for them, witnessed only by the dawn and the vast Atlantic expanse. They chose the Cliffs of Moher, and set their ceremony for 4:30 AM to catch the sunrise unfolding over the ocean.

In the quiet of early morning, with the birds waking up around them and the cliffs bathed in soft golden light, they stood at the edge of the world and said yes. It was peaceful, beautiful, and completely unbothered — exactly what they had dreamed of. The raw emotion, the stillness of the moment, and the majesty of the landscape came together to create something truly magical.

Beautifully captured by Marie O’Mahony, whose talent for finding light and emotion in every frame made their sunrise ceremony absolutely timeless.

About the Venue

Cliffs of Moher,
Co. Clare

The Cliffs of Moher rise majestically along the southwestern edge of the Burren region, standing 700 feet above the wild Atlantic Ocean. These ancient sea cliffs stretch for over 8 kilometres along the Clare coast, creating one of Ireland’s most iconic and breathtaking landscapes.

A sunrise ceremony here is not just romantic — it’s transcendent. The quiet of early morning, the soft golden light spreading across the ocean, and the sheer scale of the cliffs create an atmosphere of profound peace and natural beauty. It’s a place where the world feels vast and timeless, and saying yes feels like a promise to the universe itself.
