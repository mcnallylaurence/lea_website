Frequently asked questions.

An elopement is just the two of you — or a very small handful of your closest people (think: up to 8 guests). The focus is entirely on the ceremony itself: a meaningful moment in an extraordinary place, with no seating charts, no formal programme, and no compromises. After the vows, you might have a long, slow dinner at a beautiful restaurant. It’s intimate, unhurried, and completely yours. Our Fáilte and Grá packages are built for this.

A destination wedding is a full celebration with a proper guest list. There’s a private reception space, a coordinated day-of timeline, live music, a wedding planner managing every detail, and all the logistics that come with bringing a group of people together in a foreign country — room blocks, welcome dinners, transport, and more. Our Sláinte and Ard packages cover this. The difference isn’t just guest count — it’s the scale of coordination involved.

Not sure which is right for you? That’s exactly what the free call is for.
