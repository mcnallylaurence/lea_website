It all started at a bar named Ladybird

How a bar named Ladybird became a business built on love.

We met in Atlanta, Georgia - at a bar on the Beltline called Ladybird Grove & Mess Hall. One pint led to another, and before we knew it, we were planning our wedding.

Like a lot of couples, we initially planned to have our wedding in the States. But the more we explored that path, the more overwhelmed we felt. Laurence is from Ireland. Yasmeen is from Atlanta - and suddenly, the idea of an Irish wedding with wild cliffs, old stone churches, and pub-filled joy started calling to us.

So we planned our wedding 4,000 miles away on the west coast of Ireland. It was beautiful. It was chaotic. It was unforgettable. And it made us realise: there has to be an easier way.

Ladybird Ever After was born from that experience.
