The Planning Roadmap

Whether you book us two years out or two months before your wedding, the approach is the same: lock in the big pieces immediately, then pace the rest so everything happens in the right order. Some things-like booking coach transfers-cannot be done too far in advance because schedules change. Here is the exact sequence.

Immediately After You Book

Discovery Call and Venue: Our very first step is a call to understand your vision so we can identify and lock in your venue. Everything else-vendors, logistics, timeline-flows from the venue. This happens before anything else.

Primary Vendors: Once the venue is confirmed, we immediately secure your high-demand vendors, such as your photographer and your celebrant. We will formally introduce you and securely share your information with them. You do not need to think about the finer details just yet-your primary vendors will reach out to you directly when the timing is right for them to begin planning the specifics of your day.

The 12-Month Mark

Guest Communication: For larger parties, this is when you should start gathering guest addresses and sending save-the-dates. Your guests need maximum lead time for international travel-the earlier they know, the more likely they are to commit and the better the airfares they can find.

The 9-Month Mark

Legal Paperwork: If you are having a legally binding wedding in Ireland, this is when you file your notification with the Civil Registration Service. Starting at nine months gives you significant buffer against any administrative delays or document issues.

The 6-Month Mark

Hair and Makeup: We lock in your glam team.

Hard RSVP Cutoff: We need your final, confirmed headcount six months out. This number drives every remaining decision: your dinner setup, seating plan, catering quantities, and final vendor logistics. Treat this deadline seriously.

The 4 to 5-Month Mark

Formal Invitations: Send your formal invites with all the final details your guests need: venue address, accommodation suggestions, travel tips, and the schedule for the wedding day (in broad strokes).

Attire and Rings: Finalize your wedding rings. Begin dress alterations and suit fittings. This takes longer than most people expect-especially if your dress needs multiple fittings.

The 3-Month Mark

High-Level Day Timeline: We build the structural flow of your wedding day: ceremony time, transportation windows, dinner timing, key moments. We cannot build an accurate minute-by-minute timeline any further in advance than this, so this is when the real shape of your day comes into focus.

Vow Building: You begin working closely with your celebrant on your ceremony script, personal vows, readings, and any symbolic rituals you want to include.

The 1 to 3-Month Mark

Ceremony Script: You finalize the exact wording of your ceremony-every reading, every declaration, your music selections.

One Month Out

The Final Timeline: Exactly one month before your wedding, we deliver your precise minute-by-minute timeline for the day. Every vendor knows where to be and when. Every transfer is booked. Every detail is confirmed.

From here, your job is simple: pack your bags and get on the plane. We handle everything else.
