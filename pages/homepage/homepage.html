Our Process

Getting married in Ireland is easier than you think.

1

Tell us what you're dreaming of

A free discovery call with your planner. We listen to your vision, answer every question, and match you to the right package, venue, and team.

2

We build your wedding from the ground up

Venue secured, vendors booked, legal paperwork filed, timeline built - all coordinated by us, all priced in USD, all managed from the US side of the Atlantic.

3

You land in Ireland with nothing left to do

Every detail is in place before you board your flight. Your planner is on the ground, your vendors are briefed, and your day is ready.

4

Get married in one of the most beautiful places on earth

Stand where you always pictured it - castle, cliff, countryside, or coast - and say the words that matter. We’ll take care of everything else.
