Their Story

Just the two of them, choosing Ireland for the most magical yes.

Jessica and Kyle knew exactly what they wanted: to come to Ireland and get married, just the two of them. Travelling all the way from Pennsylvania, they put their trust in Ladybird Ever After to plan every detail of their intimate elopement — from the perfect location to the celebrant, photographer, and everything in between.

Ross Castle, standing on the edge of Lough Leane in Killarney National Park, gave them everything they had dreamed of. Ancient stone towers, mountain views, and the kind of peaceful, unhurried beauty that makes you feel the world has fallen away entirely.

Beautifully photographed by Dylan Clifford, whose talent for capturing authentic emotion made every moment timeless.

About the Venue

Ross Castle,
Co. Kerry

Ross Castle is a 15th-century tower house and keep standing on the edge of Lough Leane, within the breathtaking surroundings of Killarney National Park. One of Ireland’s most iconic and photographed landmarks, its ancient stone walls rising from the lakeshore create an incomparably romantic setting for an intimate elopement.

With sweeping views across the lake to the MacGillycuddy’s Reeks mountain range and the tranquillity of the surrounding woodland and wildlife, Ross Castle is a place of quiet, timeless beauty — exactly the kind of setting that turns a ceremony into a memory that lasts a lifetime.
