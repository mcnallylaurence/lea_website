Crucial: Getting Ready & Hotel Policies

N.B. DO NOT just book a hotel on Expedia for the morning of your wedding.

You cannot simply book a random hotel online and expect to have a glam squad arrive, or plan to walk through their lobby in your wedding dress and outfits on the morning of your wedding.

Many Irish hotels operate on a strict one-wedding-per-day policy. If they are hosting another wedding on your date, they will absolutely not allow a different bridal party to use their facilities to get ready.

If you book a hotel that does not allow you to get ready, Ladybird cannot provide services for your wedding, such as hair and makeup, unless we have explicitly communicated with and received clearance from the hotel team.

It is very important that we work through the booking of your accommodation for the night before and the night of your wedding together. If you are ever in doubt about hotel policies, booking an Airbnb is always the safest option for your wedding morning.

The Core Room Block

If your venue has on-site accommodation, we will work with the venue to prioritize rooms for you, your immediate family, and your wedding party. The venue will hold these rooms for a set period before releasing them to the general public. We will give you the specific booking deadline you need to hit. Do not miss it-once those rooms go back into general availability, you cannot get them back.

Self-Catering Cottages and Airbnbs

As mentioned above, if you aren't staying at your primary venue, getting ready at an Airbnb or self-catering accommodation is highly recommended. It guarantees you the space and freedom you need on your wedding morning without worrying about hotel restrictions. Just confirm the practical details in advance: is there enough light, enough space, and enough mirrors? We can help you think through the logistics.

For groups of friends or larger families traveling together, renting these properties can also be very cost-effective and enjoyable. Ireland has a strong supply of well-equipped self-catering homes, particularly in rural and coastal areas.

Important note for guests booking Airbnbs: Tell them to check the actual map distance to the venue, not just the town name. In Ireland, an Airbnb that looks nearby on a listing can be 15 to 20 minutes down a narrow country lane with no reliable taxi service. Staying within walking distance of the main town-and the pubs-is always the smarter choice.

Irish Bed and Breakfasts

Encourage your guests to stay at Irish B&Bs. The American idea of a B&B-a dusty Victorian house with someone else's antiques everywhere-does not apply here. Irish B&Bs are often purpose-built, carefully regulated, extremely comfortable, and they serve breakfast that most guests describe as one of the best meals of their trip. They are the backbone of Irish hospitality and a genuinely wonderful experience for first-time visitors.
