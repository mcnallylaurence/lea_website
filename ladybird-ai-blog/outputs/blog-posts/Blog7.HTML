Why Atlanta Couples Are Choosing Ireland

There are some practical advantages that make Ireland especially appealing for Georgia couples:

  • Direct flights from Atlanta to Dublin. Aer Lingus and Delta both fly direct, 7-8 hours. No connections, no hassle. You leave Atlanta Friday evening and you're in Dublin Saturday morning.
  • Same planner, same timezone. You're working with us. We're in Atlanta. Meetings happen in EST, not Irish time. That's a huge advantage over hiring an Ireland-based planner you've never met.
  • Everything is priced in USD. One invoice. No currency surprises. No wire transfers to Irish banks. One American company, one contract, predictable costs.
  • Ireland is a vacation destination. It's not just a ceremony; it's a trip. You're not flying to a remote island with nothing to do between events. You can explore Dublin, hike the Wild Atlantic Way, drink Guinness in authentic pubs, and have genuine adventures.
  • The cost is often comparable. An Atlanta venue rental + catering + photographer can easily match an all-inclusive Ireland elopement package. But you get the trip on top of the ceremony.

Atlanta has great elopement options. But Ireland offers something different: it's an adventure, a trip, and a ceremony all rolled into one.

FAQs From Atlanta Couples

Is It Legally Recognized in the US?

Yes, absolutely. A marriage conducted in Ireland by a registered officiant is legally recognized in all 50 states. You'll come home as a legally married couple. No need for additional paperwork or registration in Georgia. Read our full legal guide for the details.

How Far in Advance Do I Need to Book?

Nine to twelve months is ideal. The HSE (Ireland's health authority) requires 3 months notice minimum for marriage notification. But vendors book up, flights are cheaper when you book ahead, and you'll have more flexibility with locations. That said, we've coordinated elopements with shorter timelines.

Can My Family Come?

Absolutely. If you want just the two of you, that's perfect. If you want to bring 10 family members, we have packages for that too. Our Slainte package is perfect for 13-25 guests and has the highest close rate of all our packages. Or bring a few people and keep it intimate. It's your choice.
