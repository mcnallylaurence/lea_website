Their Story

A dream reality unfolding like magic.

Sebrina and John traveled all the way from Kentucky with a dream of a magical, unforgettable elopement in Ireland. They were an absolute dream to work with from day one, bringing endless warmth and joy to the planning journey as they readied for the breathtaking cliffs of the western coast.

On their wedding day, the forecast promised heavy rain, and it poured relentlessly all morning. But as if by pure magic, the skies completely parted and cleared right as Sebrina and John stepped up to say their vows over the wild Atlantic edge. The dramatic, parting storm clouds gave way to the most beautifully surreal lighting, bringing their fairytale perfectly to life.

About the Location

Cliffs of Moher,
Co. Clare

Towering over the wild Atlantic Ocean, the Cliffs of Moher in County Clare are one of Ireland's most spectacular and globally recognized natural wonders. Stretching for miles along the western coastline, these iconic cliffs provide an awe-inspiring, sweeping backdrop of rugged perfection for an elopement.

Couples seeking dramatic tension and unbridled romance will find the sweeping panoramas and crashing waves beneath them to be utterly unforgettable. It stands as a timeless marker of Ireland's raw, elemental beauty.
