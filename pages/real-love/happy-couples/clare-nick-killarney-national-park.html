A smooth, stress-free, and unforgettable experience in Killarney.

Shortly after getting engaged, Clare and Nick knew they wanted to escape the traditional hustle and bustle to have an intimate celebration in Ireland. Drawn to the sweeping landscapes and ancient ruins of the South West, they chose the majestic Killarney National Park for their spectacular day.

With a vision of a quaint and gorgeous Irish wedding, they entrusted the Ladybird team to pull together the details. From seamless vendor coordination to thoughtful travel advice and indispensable on-the-day support—including safeguarding the veil from the wind!—Clare and Nick were able to share in a truly magical, stress-free elopement alongside their closest family members.

About the Location

Killarney National Park,
Co. Kerry

Killarney National Park in County Kerry is a breathtaking expanse of rugged mountains, pristine lakes, and ancient woodlands. As Ireland's first national park, it provides a secluded, mystical atmosphere for an intimate elopement, while the nearby Muckross Abbey offers a stunning, historic backdrop for unforgettable photos.

Couples drawn to this region can experience the timeless beauty of the South West, from horse-drawn carriage rides to quiet lakeside vows surrounded by nature. It's an unforgettable setting for a wedding day deeply connected to Ireland's rich history and landscape.
