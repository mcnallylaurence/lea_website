Their Story

About the Venue

Kinnitty Castle,
Co. Offaly

Kinnitty Castle is a stunning Gothic Revival castle set on 650 acres of parkland in the heart of Ireland's midlands. Dating back to the early 1200s, the castle has been lovingly restored into a luxury hotel and wedding venue that combines medieval grandeur with modern comfort.

With its dramatic stone towers, grand reception rooms, roaring fireplaces, and manicured grounds backed by the Slieve Bloom Mountains, Kinnitty is one of Ireland's most photogenic castle wedding venues and one of the few where you can stay overnight in the castle itself.
