Their Story

Absolute perfection on the Dingle Peninsula.

Mikel and Brian dreamed of a breathtaking Irish wedding but didn’t want the grueling effort of planning it themselves. Coming over from Maryland while Mikel was finishing her PhD, they trusted the Ladybird Ever After team to meticulously organize their micro wedding from afar.

The day unfolded beautifully. They shared an unforgettable, breathtaking ceremony amidst the historic ruins of Rahinnane Castle, surrounded by their closest family and friends. Afterwards, the party continued with a lively DJ reception at the stunning Dingle Skellig Hotel, overlooking Dingle Bay. Ladybird ensured every little detail, from the florist to the venue staff, was perfectly aligned with their vision, allowing Mikel and Brian to be fully present and soak in every second of their perfect day.

About the Location

Dingle Skellig Hotel & Rahinnane Castle,
Co. Kerry

Situated in the heart of the wild South West, this incredible double venue combination offers couples a majestic blend of ancient history and coastal luxury. The dramatic ruins of Rahinnane Castle provide a breathtaking backdrop for a truly unforgettable and deeply romantic ceremony.

Following the vows, a short scenic journey brings you to the Dingle Skellig Hotel, a premier coastal destination overlooking the magnificent Dingle Bay. With its world-class hospitality, sweeping sea views, and outstanding amenities, it perfectly completes a magical Irish wedding experience on the Dingle Peninsula.
