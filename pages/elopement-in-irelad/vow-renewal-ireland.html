What we offer

A ceremony that honours your story

A vow renewal is one of the most personal things you can do — and the most flexible. Unlike a legal wedding, there are no forms to file and no legal requirements to navigate. This is purely about meaning, intention, and the two of you choosing each other again.

We design every vow renewal ceremony from scratch, shaped entirely around your story. The setting you've always dreamed of, the words that reflect where you are now, the small details that make the whole day feel like it was made only for you.

Vow renewals can be entirely private — just the two of you and a photographer — or a full celebration with family and close friends, a reception dinner, and an evening that spills naturally into the Irish night.

Kerry · Cork

Vow renewals in

The South West

For a vow renewal that feels cinematic and deeply romantic, the south west is hard to surpass. Lakeside castles, ancient woodland, mountain reflections on glassy water — Kerry and Cork offer an Ireland that feels like it was designed for love stories.

The Gulf Stream keeps the south west lush and temperate year-round, making it a beautiful choice even outside summer. A garden ceremony in November, a woodland walk in February — the south west holds its beauty in every season.

Explore the South West
Popular for vow renewals
  • Lakeside castle settings for intimate or celebratory ceremonies
  • Killarney National Park's ancient woodland and glassy lakes
  • Year-round lush greenery from the Gulf Stream climate
  • Private gardens and walled courtyards
  • The Ring of Kerry for a romantic itinerary around the ceremony
  • Exceptional restaurants in Killarney and Kenmare for the celebration

The warmth and gentleness of the south east — both its climate and its character — makes it a beautiful choice for a vow renewal. Kilkenny's medieval streets, the stillness of Tintern Abbey's woodland estuary, and the sweep of Wexford's coastline offer very different but equally moving settings.
