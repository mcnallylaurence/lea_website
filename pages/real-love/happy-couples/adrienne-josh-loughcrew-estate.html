Their Story

A full Irish countryside experience, from the house to the final campfire toast.

Adrienne and Josh came all the way from the US with their nearest and dearest for an all-inclusive celebration in the Irish countryside. They chose Loughcrew Estate - a stunning manor house set within peaceful parkland - as the backdrop for their wedding weekend.

From the intimate ceremony to the flowing reception, they experienced every moment fully immersed in the beauty of the Irish landscape. The highlight of their celebration? An evening campfire where they toasted to love, laughter, and the magic of bringing everyone they cherish to a place as beautiful as this. It was a wedding that felt like coming home, and staying as long as the embers glowed.

Beautifully documented by Olga Hogan, whose eye for detail and warmth captures the true joy of celebration.

About the Venue

Loughcrew Estate,
Co. Meath

Loughcrew Estate is a stunning country house set within sprawling parkland in the heart of the Irish Midlands. The estate captures the timeless elegance of Irish countryside living, with beautiful grounds that feel both intimate and expansive. It’s the perfect place for couples who want to celebrate surrounded by natural beauty and have the freedom to make the entire weekend their own.

Whether gathered in the graceful halls of the house or celebrating under the stars with a campfire glowing in the darkness, Loughcrew creates moments that feel timeless. It’s a setting where love stories unfold slowly, intimately, and beautifully - exactly as they should.
