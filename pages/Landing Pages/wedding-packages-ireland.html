Popular months (May through September) book out 12 or more months in advance. We recommend reaching out as early as possible to secure your preferred date, especially if you have a specific venue in mind. We understand that plans change. Our team will work with you to find an alternative date and coordinate with all vendors. Date change policies vary depending on how close you are to the original wedding date -- your US planner will walk you through the specific terms during your consultation.
US couples must file a Notification of Intention to Marry with an Irish registrar at least 3 months before the ceremony. You do not need to be resident in Ireland -- you only need to file the notification in person at a registry office, which is typically done on arrival in Ireland. Every Ladybird package includes full guidance on this process. We prepare your paperwork, brief you on exactly what to bring, and accompany you to your registry appointment if needed.
