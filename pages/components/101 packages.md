**Can Americans legally get married in Ireland?**

Yes. Ireland is one of the most welcoming countries in the world for international couples to get legally married. There is no residency requirement, meaning you do not need to be an Irish citizen or live in Ireland to marry there. You will need to give three months' notice to the HSE (Health Service Executive) and provide certain documents. We handle all of this guidance as part of every package. [Read our complete legal guide →] (link to /blog/how-to-get-married-in-ireland)

---

**What documents do I need to get married in Ireland as a US citizen?**

You will need a valid US passport, your birth certificate, and if applicable, a divorce decree or death certificate of a former spouse. You will also need to complete the notification process with Ireland's HSE at least three months before your ceremony date. Our team walks you through every step of this process and reviews your paperwork before submission. [Read our full documentation guide →] (link to /blog/getting-married-in-ireland-as-an-american)

---

**What is the best time of year to elope in Ireland?**

Ireland is beautiful year-round, and we plan elopements in every season. The most popular months are May through September, when you get the longest daylight hours (sunset can be as late as 10pm in June) and milder temperatures. However, autumn and winter elopements have a dramatic beauty all their own — golden light, moody skies, and far fewer tourists at popular locations. [Read our seasonal weather guide →] (link to /blog/eloping-in-ireland-weather-what-to-expect-in-every-season)

---

**Do you only plan elopements, or can you plan larger weddings in Ireland?**

We plan everything from two-person elopements to intimate weddings of 50+ guests. Our Sláinte, Ard, and RíRá packages include full reception coordination and wedding planning. Whether your celebration is a quiet ceremony on a clifftop followed by pints in a pub, or a formal dinner in a castle ballroom, we tailor the experience to your vision and guest count.

---

**How far in advance should I book my Ireland elopement?**

We recommend booking 6 to 12 months in advance, especially if you have a specific date or popular venue in mind. However, we have successfully planned elopements in as little as 3 months. The key constraint is Ireland's legal requirement of 3 months' notice before the ceremony, so that is the absolute minimum timeline for a legal marriage.
